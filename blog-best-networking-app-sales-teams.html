What Sales Teams Need in a Networking App

Not every contact management tool is built for the way sales teams actually work at events. Here's what matters:

  • Speed of capture — At a busy event, you have 30 seconds between conversations. The app needs to let you save a contact in under 10 seconds.
  • Automatic enrichment — A name and company isn't enough. You need job title, LinkedIn profile, company size, and recent news to personalize follow-ups.
  • Smart reminders — Not just "follow up in 3 days" but contextual reminders based on what you discussed and what's relevant now.
  • Team sharing — If three people from your company attend the same event, everyone should benefit from every contact captured.
  • CRM integration — Contacts need to flow into your existing pipeline, not live in a separate silo.
